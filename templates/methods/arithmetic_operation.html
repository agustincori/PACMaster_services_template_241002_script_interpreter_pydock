POST /arithmetic_operation

Description: This route performs an arithmetic operation between two numbers and saves the arguments and the result.

Parameters:

  • arg1: The first number (int or float)
  • arg2: The second number (int or float)
  • operation: The arithmetic operation to perform (string). Possible values: 'sum', 'diff', 'mult', 'div', 'pwr', 'root'
